About Commbot

Crises like Covid-19 comes with no or little warning and bring great personal, physical, financial, and emotional burdens.

It’s during these times people needed helping hands. Communities has to respond quickly to offer support and help those affected return to life in a new normal.

Our solution analyse the each need of customer and derive insights out of it and pass it to the government . This will help Government bodies to see what the needs are, how they can help with and stay updated on changing needs, conditions, and circumstances.
